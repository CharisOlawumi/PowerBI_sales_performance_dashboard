## üìå Introduction

This project is a **Sales Performance Dashboard** developed in **Power BI** using a grocery retail dataset.  
The dashboard provides insights into **sales performance, product trends, customer behavior, and geographic distribution**, with an emphasis on interactivity and business decision support.

The project was built as a **portfolio piece** to demonstrate practical skills in **Power BI, DAX, and Power Query**, covering the full analytics workflow from data preparation to insight delivery.

## üß≠ Dashboard Overview

### **Page 1 ‚Äî Main/Executive Overview**
![Dashboard Page 1](/images/Page1.png)

The main dashboard presents a high-level view of business performance, focusing on key metrics such as **total revenue, total orders, and units sold**. It highlights overall sales trends through **monthly revenue analysis**, supported by trend lines and KPIs to track performance over time. Product categories and top-performing products are emphasized to quickly identify major revenue and order drivers.

### **Page 2 ‚Äî Geographic Analysis**
![Dashboard Page 1](/images/Page2.png)

This page visualizes **total orders by U.S. cities** using a map, allowing users to quickly assess geographic concentration and regional demand patterns. It provides a spatial perspective on sales activity across different locations.

### **Page 3 ‚Äî Product Analysis**
![Dashboard Page 1](/images/Page3.png)

The product analysis page enables deeper exploration of individual product performance. Users can select a product to analyze **orders and revenue trends over time**, compare performance against targets using gauges, and simulate pricing changes through interactive parameters. This page supports data-driven product and pricing decisions.

### **Page 4 ‚Äî Customer Insights**
![Dashboard Page 1](/images/Page4.png)

The customer-focused page examines **customer value and behavior**, highlighting unique customers, average revenue per customer, and customer growth trends over time. It also identifies top customers by revenue and order volume, providing insight into high-value customer segments.
